Scan your AI visibility across ChatGPT, Claude & Gemini. Auto-deploy llms.txt, inject schema markup, and get actionable fixes to improve how AI describes your brand.

== Description ==

**ShowsUp** is the easiest way for WordPress site owners to improve how AI models — ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini — describe and recommend their brand.

=== What ShowsUp Does ===

* **AI Visibility Score** — Scan your site and get a score out of 100 showing how well AI models represent your brand across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Claude.
* **llms.txt Auto-Deploy** — Generate and deploy a `llms.txt` file at `yoursite.com/llms.txt` in one click. This structured file tells AI crawlers exactly what your site is about.
* **Schema Markup Injection** — Automatically inject Organisation, FAQ, and WooCommerce Product schema into your site's `<head>` to help AI models understand your content.
* **AI-Powered Fixes** — Get a prioritised list of actionable fixes generated by the ShowsUp AI Analyst based on your scan data.
* **Gutenberg Sidebar** — Check your AI visibility score and redeploy llms.txt without leaving the block editor.
* **Weekly Auto-Scan** — Automatically re-scan your site on a schedule and receive email notifications when results are ready.
* **Dashboard Widget** — See your current AI Visibility Score at a glance on the WordPress admin dashboard.

A free or paid ShowsUp account and API token from [showsup.co](https://showsup.co).

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `showsup` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory, or install the plugin through the **Plugins > Add New** screen in WordPress.
2. Activate the plugin through the **Plugins** screen.
3. Navigate to **ShowsUp** in your WordPress admin menu.
4. Go to **Settings** and enter your API token from [showsup.co](https://showsup.co).
5. Click **Run First Scan** on the Overview tab.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where do I get an API token? =

Sign up at [showsup.co](https://showsup.co). Free scans are included on all plans.

= What is llms.txt? =

`llms.txt` is an emerging standard (similar to `robots.txt`) that helps AI crawlers understand your site's content and purpose. ShowsUp auto-generates it from your WordPress content and scan data.

= Does this slow down my site? =

No. Schema injection adds a small `<script>` block to your `<head>`. The llms.txt file is served via a WordPress rewrite rule with a 24-hour cache. All scanning happens in the background via WP Cron.

= Does ShowsUp work with WooCommerce? =

Yes. ShowsUp automatically injects WooCommerce Product schema on product pages, including price, availability, and aggregate rating.

= Can I self-host ShowsUp? =

Yes. Set a custom Cloud URL in the Settings tab pointing to your self-hosted ShowsUp instance.

= Does this replace an SEO plugin? =

No. ShowsUp is focused on AI Engine Optimisation (AEO) — how AI models describe your brand — rather than traditional search engine ranking. It works alongside Yoast SEO, Rank Math, or any other SEO plugin.

= Will the schema conflict with my SEO plugin? =

If you already inject Organisation or Product schema via Yoast, Rank Math, or a similar plugin, you can disable ShowsUp's schema injection in **Settings → Schema Markup** to avoid duplicate markup.
